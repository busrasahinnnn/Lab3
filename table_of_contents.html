Titles Dates Brief
Caipirinha 08/05/2023 I made my first Caipirinha on May,8th 2023. It is a famous Brezilian drink. It contain half lime, two spoons of sugar, 1 oz lemon juice, one scoop of ice, 1 1/4 oz cachaca.
Sex on the Beach 23/05/2023 I made my first Sex on the Beach on May,23th 2023. The smooth cocktail was invented in the 1980s by Ted Pizio, a bartender in Florida. It contain one scoop of ice, 1 1/4 vodka, 1 oz peach scnapps, orange juice, cranberry juice, little bit of granada.
Espresso Martini 09/06/2023 I made my first Espresso Martini on June,6th 2023. English bartender Dick Bradsell invented this delicious drink. It contain one scoop of ice, 1 oz vodka, 1 oz baileys, 1 oz kahlua, one double espresso.
Margarita 10/06/2023 I made my first Margarita on June,10th 2023. This delicious drink was discovered by Mexican Pancho Morales in the summer of 1942. It contain half scoop of ice, 1 1/4 tequila, 1 oz grand marnier or triple sec, 1 oz lemon juice, salted rim.
